hi i'm belinda

i was born in los angeles, raised in taiwan, and am now in california for university. as a graphic design enthusiast and self-taught computer programmer, i’ve always adored the connections between technology and design made possible by the ceaseless innovations in our ever-changing world.

i picked up computer coding as a hobby towards the end of elementary school. while i fawned over one direction as my classmates did, i also had the apple keynote speeches presented by steve jobs and jony ives playing on repeat. from sketching out floor plans and interior designs while maximizing the functions of each component to simply coding small projects and transitions, i’ve always had an aptitude for combining innumerable forms of design and cutting-edge technology.

i am currently a sophomore double majoring in cognitive science and data science at the university of southern california. stating that i’m interested in bridging artful conception and innovation is an understatement; it is what i’ve dedicated my time to for years.
